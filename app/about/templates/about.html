My goals

Who am I?

I was born and raised in Hungary, Europe. I've moved to Malta to experience other cultures as well I'd like to have an open mind and travelling helps me achieving this :) So far I have worked as Machine Learning Engineer for a partner company of Deutsche Telekom, then I started to work as a DevOps Specialist for the biggest telco company in Malta. I also have an MSc of Electrical Engineering so the statistics and math behind Data Science wont scare me away!

Experience
